Our History

Fauji Security Services (Pvt) Limited, (FSS) is a subsidiary of Army Welfare Trust. Considered as one of the most trusted security partners in Public as well as Private Sector, it is providing Security Services all over Pakistan including Azad Jammu & Kashmir since 1993. 80% of our manpower comprises of retired Armed Forces Personnel. The Company was taken over from Fauji Foundation by Army Welfare Trust in 2013. In 2015 the Company has undergone major revamping and now it is one of the major Security Solutions providers in Pakistan. Our Security Services include various types of Guarding Services, Customized Technical Security Solutions, Cash-In-Transit (CIT) Services and Consultancy & Training. Ours is one of the lead Security Companies designated by the Ministry of Interior for imparting training to Security Guards of other Private Security Companies.
